❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert UTC to PST?
Subtract 8 hours from any UTC time. If the result is negative, add 24 and it's the previous calendar day. UTC 17:00 − 8 = PST 9:00 AM.
What is UTC 17:00 in PST?
UTC 17:00 = PST 9:00 AM. 17 − 8 = 9 AM PST. This is a common developer reference time (9 AM Pacific = 5 PM UTC).
What is UTC noon in PST?
UTC 12:00 (noon) = PST 4:00 AM. 12 − 8 = 4 AM. UTC noon is very early morning in California.
What is UTC midnight in PST?
UTC 00:00 = PST 4:00 PM (the previous day). 0 − 8 = −8, +24 = 16:00 = 4 PM previous day.
