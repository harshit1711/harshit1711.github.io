🧮 The Formula

The offset between IST and EST is straightforward:

IST → EST: subtract 10 hours 30 minutes (EST winter) or 9 hours 30 minutes (EDT summer)
EST → IST: add 10 hours 30 minutes (EST winter) or 9 hours 30 minutes (EDT summer)
📌 IST = UTC+5:30 • EST = UTC-5 • EDT = UTC-4
Difference in winter (EST): IST is 10h 30m ahead of EST
Difference in summer (EDT): IST is 9h 30m ahead of EDT
India never changes its clocks. Only the US offset changes.

⚠️ Daylight Saving Time — When the Offset Changes

The IST–EST offset is not constant year-round. Here's why:

  • India is always at UTC+5:30 — the clocks never change
  • The US East Coast switches between EST (UTC-5) in winter and EDT (UTC-4) in summer
  • When the US "springs forward" (second Sunday of March), the IST–EST gap shrinks by 1 hour to 9h 30m
  • When the US "falls back" (first Sunday of November), the gap returns to 10h 30m

💻 For Developers — IST/EST in Code

Handling IST to EST in software requires timezone-aware datetime libraries. Using raw offsets (+5:30 or -5:00) will break when DST changes. Always use IANA timezone IDs:

# Python
from zoneinfo import ZoneInfo
from datetime import datetime
ist = datetime.now(ZoneInfo("Asia/Kolkata"))
est = ist.astimezone(ZoneInfo("America/New_York"))

// JavaScript
const ist = new Date().toLocaleString("en-US", {timeZone: "Asia/Kolkata"});
const est = new Date().toLocaleString("en-US", {timeZone: "America/New_York"});

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the time difference between IST and EST?

IST is 10 hours 30 minutes ahead of EST (UTC-5) in winter. During US Daylight Saving Time (EDT, UTC-4), the gap reduces to 9 hours 30 minutes. India never changes its clocks.

What is 9 AM IST in EST?

9:00 AM IST = 10:30 PM EST (previous day). Subtract 10h 30m from the IST time to get EST.

What is 9 AM EST in IST?

9:00 AM EST = 7:30 PM IST (same day). Add 10h 30m to the EST time to get IST. During EDT (summer), 9 AM EDT = 6:30 PM IST.

Why does India use UTC+5:30 instead of a whole-hour offset?

India chose UTC+5:30 based on the 82.5°E meridian that runs through the geographic center of the country. This half-hour offset is shared with Sri Lanka and Nepal region. See our full explainer: Why IST is UTC+5:30.

Is IST the same as EDT?

No — IST (UTC+5:30) and EDT (UTC-4) are not the same timezone. IST is 9 hours 30 minutes ahead of EDT. They are separate timezones on different continents.
