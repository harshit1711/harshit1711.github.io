Frequently Asked Questions

🔹 How do I convert UTC to IST (Indian Standard Time)?

To convert UTC to IST, simply add 5 hours and 30 minutes. For example, 12:00 UTC is 17:30 IST. Use the converter above to get accurate results instantly.

🔹 How do I convert local time to UTC?

Enter your local time in the format YYYY-MM-DD HH:mm:ss into the tool and click "Convert to UTC". The tool automatically adjusts for your system’s timezone offset.

🔹 What is UTC time used for?

UTC (Coordinated Universal Time) is the world’s time standard. It is widely used in software development, aviation, astronomy, finance, and global business communication.

🔹 What is the difference between UTC and GMT?

GMT (Greenwich Mean Time) is a time zone, while UTC is a time standard. In practice, they are often the same, but UTC is more precise and is used globally in computing and communication systems.

🔹 Can I convert UTC to my country’s timezone?

Yes! Enter your UTC time in the converter, and it will instantly show your local timezone time, whether you’re in IST, EST, PST, CET, or any other region.

🔹 Why should developers use UTC?

Storing and processing timestamps in UTC avoids confusion with daylight savings and different local times. This ensures consistency across servers and databases worldwide.
