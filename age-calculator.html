Exact Age Calculator

Enter your date of birth to instantly see your exact age in years, months, days, total days lived, total hours, and days until your next birthday.

How is age calculated?

Age is calculated by counting the full years, months, and days between your date of birth and today. A year is counted only when the full calendar year has passed. The total days and hours are calculated from the exact timestamp of midnight on your birthday to the current moment.
