💼 How Indian IT Companies Handle the Overlap Problem

India's massive IT and BPO industry has developed structured approaches to the overlap problem:

  • Morning shift (US EST): 6:30 AM ‚Äì 3:30 PM IST ‚Äî aligns with US East Coast late evening/night
  • General shift: 9:00 AM ‚Äì 6:00 PM IST ‚Äî standard India business hours, minimal US overlap
  • Evening shift (US PST): 6:00 PM ‚Äì 3:00 AM IST ‚Äî aligns with US West Coast full business day
  • Night shift: 9:00 PM ‚Äì 6:00 AM IST ‚Äî covers US East Coast full business day
  • Rotating "sacrifice": Some teams alternate who takes the early/late hit week by week
