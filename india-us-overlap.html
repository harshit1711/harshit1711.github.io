🗓 India–US Work Hours Overlap

Find exactly when India and the US work at the same time — and the best windows for standups, client calls, and meetings.

WorkIndia › India–US Work Hours Overlap

India–US collaboration is one of the largest cross-timezone working relationships in the world — yet the time difference makes scheduling genuinely hard. India (IST, UTC+5:30) is 10.5 to 13.5 hours ahead of the US depending on which coast, meaning there is almost no natural business-hours overlap. This guide explains every overlap window and how to make it work.

⚠️ The hard truth: When India's business day runs 9 AM–6 PM IST, the US East Coast time is 10:30 PM–7:30 AM EST — entirely overnight. There is no natural overlap. Both sides must sacrifice some comfort.

💼 How Indian IT Companies Handle the Overlap Problem

India's massive IT and BPO industry has developed structured approaches to the overlap problem:

  • Morning shift (US EST): 6:30 AM – 3:30 PM IST — aligns with US East Coast late evening/night
  • General shift: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM IST — standard India business hours, minimal US overlap
  • Evening shift (US PST): 6:00 PM – 3:00 AM IST — aligns with US West Coast full business day
  • Night shift: 9:00 PM – 6:00 AM IST — covers US East Coast full business day
  • Rotating "sacrifice": Some teams alternate who takes the early/late hit week by week

⚠️ Impact of US Daylight Saving Time on India Overlap

When the US shifts clocks for DST, the overlap window moves by 1 hour. India never changes its clocks, so:

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any overlap between India and US business hours?

Almost none naturally. India (9 AM–6 PM IST) corresponds to 10:30 PM–7:30 AM EST — entirely outside US business hours. Overlap requires one side to adjust: India works early mornings (7–9 AM IST) or evenings (6–11 PM IST).

What is the best meeting time for India and US teams?

For India–EST: 8:00–9:00 AM IST (9:30–10:30 PM EST). For India–PST: 9:30–10:30 PM IST (8:00–9:00 AM PST). Use our Meeting Planner for precise scheduling.

How many hours is India ahead of the US?

India (IST, UTC+5:30) is 10h 30m ahead of EST, 11h 30m ahead of CST, 12h 30m ahead of MST, and 13h 30m ahead of PST in standard time (winter). These gaps reduce by 1 hour during US summer (DST).

What is the India–US overlap for a 1-hour meeting?

The only realistic 1-hour meeting window where both India and US East are in reasonable hours is 8:00–9:00 AM IST / 9:30–10:30 PM EST. For US West Coast, 9:30–10:30 PM IST / 8:00–9:00 AM PST.
