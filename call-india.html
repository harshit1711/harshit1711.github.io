India Business Hours at a Glance

India office hours are typically 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST (UTC+5:30). India does not observe Daylight Saving Time, so these hours are consistent year-round.

  • New York (EST): India open = 10:30 PM - 7:30 AM next day (late night / early morning)
  • London (GMT): India open = 3:30 AM - 12:30 PM
  • Dubai (GST): India open = 7:30 AM - 4:30 PM
  • Singapore (SGT): India open = 6:30 AM - 3:30 PM
