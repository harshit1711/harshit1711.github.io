About India-US Meeting Overlap

The best overlap window between India (IST) and the US East Coast is 7:30 AM - 11:30 AM Eastern Time (6 PM - 10 PM IST). For US West Coast, the overlap window is very narrow: 7:30 - 9:30 AM Pacific (9-11 PM IST). India does not observe Daylight Saving Time, so these windows shift by 1 hour when the US changes clocks.
