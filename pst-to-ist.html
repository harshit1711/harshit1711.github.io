❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert PST to IST?
Add 13 hours and 30 minutes to any PST time. PST 9 AM + 13:30 = IST 10:30 PM. PST 12 PM + 13:30 = IST 1:30 AM next day.
What is the PST to IST time difference?
IST is 13 hours 30 minutes ahead of PST. PST is UTC−8, IST is UTC+5:30. The total is 8 + 5.5 = 13.5 hours.
What is PST 9 AM in IST?
PST 9:00 AM = IST 10:30 PM (22:30). This is quite late in India — most offices have closed. For meetings, PST 7–8 AM (IST 8:30–9:30 PM) or PST evening (IST morning) works better.
Does the PST to IST difference change in summer?
Yes. When the US switches to PDT (Pacific Daylight Time, UTC−7), the gap becomes +12 hours 30 minutes. PDT runs from the 2nd Sunday of March to the 1st Sunday of November.
What is PST midnight in IST?
PST 12:00 AM (midnight) = IST 1:30 PM. Because IST is so far ahead, US West Coast midnight is early afternoon in India.
