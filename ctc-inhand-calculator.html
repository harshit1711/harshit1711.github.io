What Reduces In-Hand Salary

  • Employee PF contribution
  • Income tax and cess (old/new regime)
  • Professional tax (state-dependent)
  • Other monthly deductions (policy-based)
  • Payout timing for variable and bonus components

Same CTC can produce different in-hand outcomes based on payroll structure and tax regime choices.

How to Read the Output

  • Average monthly in-hand: annualized estimate spread across 12 months
  • Fixed-only month in-hand: what you see in non-bonus months
  • Payout month increment: additional inflow when bonus/variable is released
  • Annual tax + deductions: total reduction from gross components
For offer comparison, check both average monthly in-hand and fixed-only month in-hand. Candidates often overestimate cash flow when variable payout timing is ignored.

Common Mistakes During Salary Comparison

  • Comparing CTC directly without normalizing variable payouts
  • Ignoring PF and professional tax assumptions
  • Choosing tax regime without deduction planning
  • Assuming all companies use the same salary component splits
