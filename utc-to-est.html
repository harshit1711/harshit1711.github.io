❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert UTC to EST?
Subtract 5 hours from any UTC time. If the result goes below 0, add 24 hours and it's the previous day. UTC 14:00 − 5 = EST 09:00. UTC 03:00 − 5 = EST 22:00 previous day.
What is UTC 12:00 in EST?
UTC 12:00 (noon) = EST 7:00 AM. Subtract 5 hours: 12 − 5 = 7 AM EST.
What is UTC midnight in EST?
UTC 00:00 (midnight) = EST 7:00 PM (19:00) of the previous day. 00:00 − 5 = −5h = 19:00 prior day.
When should I use EDT instead of EST?
Use EDT (UTC−4) from the 2nd Sunday of March to the 1st Sunday of November. During EDT, subtract only 4 hours from UTC. Outside this period, use EST (subtract 5 hours).
What is UTC 17:00 in EST?
UTC 17:00 = EST 12:00 PM (noon). 17 − 5 = 12.
